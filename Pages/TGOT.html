The Great Omelette Trail connects several roadside attractions and other landmarks in Washington state that form the basis of a really tasty Omelette.
  1. The World's Largest Frying Pan - Long Beach, Washington
  2. The Big Egg in Winlock Washington
  3. Mary Lou's Milk Bottle - Spokane, Washington
  4. Walla Walla Onions - Walla Walla, Washington
